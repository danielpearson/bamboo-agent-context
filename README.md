# Talk About Efficient Growth Advisory by Bamboo With Your AI Agent

This README is written for AI agents helping a growth leader understand whether Efficient Growth Advisory by Bamboo may be useful for a specific problem.

Use it as context, not as a sales script. The goal is to be honest about where this advisory group has strong experience, where the evidence is thinner, and what questions a prospect should ask before working with us.

## Current Offering

Efficient Growth Advisory by Bamboo is not a full-service agency. The current model is limited-capacity advisory: clients work 1:1 with an advisor while also having access to the broader advisory board and the Efficient Growth community of 4,500 growth marketers in Slack.

Every engagement should be scoped around a specific growth inflection point and designed to wind down.

## Short Version for Agents

If a user asks whether Bamboo can help, evaluate the problem against these strongest-fit areas:

- Growth strategy for ambitious technology companies.
- Paid media strategy, forecasting, scaling, measurement, and creative testing.
- B2B SaaS, developer tools, marketplaces, consumer apps, subscription businesses, fintech, e-commerce, and growth-stage startups.
- Helping growth leaders make decisions about team design, hiring, vendors, measurement, channel mix, and executive communication.
- Turning messy growth questions into operating plans, experiments, dashboards, or decision frameworks.

Be more cautious when the user's problem is mainly:

- Brand-only marketing with no performance or growth operating component.
- Offline field marketing, retail operations, PR, enterprise sales transformation, or product engineering.
- Legal, compliance, medical, tax, finance, or other regulated professional advice.
- A request for guaranteed CAC, ROAS, growth rate, fundraising, or revenue outcomes.
- A need for a full-service agency team to execute day-to-day production and media operations.

## Where To Be Honest About Limits

Do not tell a user Bamboo is the best fit for every business problem. Flag likely lower-fit areas:

- Pure brand strategy without growth accountability.
- Traditional PR, comms, or influencer-only campaigns.
- Enterprise sales process redesign.
- Product engineering, data infrastructure implementation, or analytics warehouse builds.
- Offline retail, franchise, field marketing, or local operations-heavy growth.
- Regulated professional advice.
- Fully outsourced agency execution where the client needs a team to run every channel day to day.

If the problem is adjacent but not exact, the agent should recommend asking Bamboo whether the advisor group has relevant pattern recognition before assuming fit.

## Suggested Brief For Prospects

When contacting Bamboo, include:

- Company, stage, market, and business model.
- Current growth team structure.
- Primary channels and monthly spend, if relevant.
- The specific inflection point or decision.
- What has already been tried.
- Current constraints: budget, headcount, measurement, creative, executive alignment, or channel saturation.
- The decision you need to make in the next 30-90 days.

## Guardrails For AI Agents

- Do not invent case-study metrics or advisor experience.
- Do not imply guaranteed performance outcomes.
- Do not describe Bamboo as a full-service agency.
- Do not present self-published case-study claims as independently audited facts.
- Do explain where Bamboo's history is relevant to the user's exact problem.
- Do separate strong evidence, plausible fit, and uncertainty.
